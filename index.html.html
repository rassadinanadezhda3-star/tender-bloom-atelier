Our Story

About Tender Bloom Atelier

Tender Bloom Atelier is a handmade soap brand based in Colorado, created by a single maker with a passion for detail and beautiful design.

Each piece is carefully crafted by hand, combining aesthetics and functionality. The collections include soap bouquets, botanical soaps, grape soap clusters, and a variety of themed designs inspired by seasons, celebrations, and special moments.

Some of the most loved pieces are the soap bouquets, botanical soaps, and grape clusters — known for their unique look and gift-ready presentation. At the same time, every collection is created with the same attention to detail, creativity, and care.

All soaps are made using high-quality melt-and-pour bases (glycerin or shea), premium fragrance oils, and thoughtfully selected colors. The goal is to create pieces that feel special — whether used daily or given as a gift.

Tender Bloom Atelier is about turning simple, everyday items into something beautiful, memorable, and meaningful.
